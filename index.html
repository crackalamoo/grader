Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Why doesn't it work?
    • A: This algorithm is definitely foolproof and if it doesn't work something is wrong with the input. Make sure to read all instructions carefully. If you still can't figure it out, contact me at bd542591@ahschool.com because it might actually be my fault that the input is bad. However, in the past, the issue has usually been that the actual grading in MyBackpack is different from what's on the syllabus; you might have to talk to your teacher (especially if it can improve your grade!)
  • Q: What if a category is missing?
    • A: If a category is missing, enter its normal percentage and everything will be ok. For example, if there is no test grade in for AP Lang, enter 5% for MC Quizzes, 25% for Quizzes, and 20% for Homework.
  • Q: Can you add my class?
    • A: Yes. There is a form to do this when you choose a class. Alternatively, contact me and give me the grading scale for the class, the name of the class, and the exact names of the categories in MyBackpack.
  • Q: Why did you make this?
    • A: I noticed people taking too long to calculate their grades and decided to ease their pain using my mad skillz.
  • Q: How did you make this?
    • A: I used a combination of HTML (to make it exist), CSS (to make it aesthetically pleasing), and JavaScript (to make it work). JavaScript is a superior language to Java, from which it is entirely separate. Anyone who argues Java is better than JavaScript without referencing Minecraft is objectively wrong.
  • Q: How do you know so many languages?
    • A: I don't actually, I would say I know about three languages (English, Hindi-Urdu, and Spanish). But this page is available in many languages and dialects and registers, so it seems like I know a lot more. I used the internet a lot to help me with the translations, so I am open to any corrections from people who speak these languages and dialects. However, I didn't just copy and paste the whole thing into Google Translate. I used the internet as a tool, but the translations are my own.
