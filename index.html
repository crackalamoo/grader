Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Why doesn't it work?
    • A: This algorithm is definitely foolproof and if it doesn't work something is wrong with the input. Make sure to read all instructions carefully. If you still can't figure it out, contact me at harysdalvi@gmail.com because it might actually be my fault that the input is bad. However, in the past, the issue has usually been that the actual grading in MyBackpack is different from what's on the syllabus; you might have to talk to your teacher (especially if it can improve your grade!)
  • Q: What if a category is missing?
    • A: If a category is missing, enter its normal percentage and everything will be ok.
  • Q: Can you add my class?
    • A: Yes. There is a form to do this when you choose a class. Alternatively, contact me and give me the grading scale for the class, the name of the class, and the exact names of the categories in MyBackpack.
  • Q: Why did you make this?
    • A: I noticed people taking too long to calculate their grades and decided to ease their pain using my mad skillz.
  • Q: How did you make this?
    • A: I used a combination of HTML (to make it exist), CSS (to make it aesthetically pleasing), and JavaScript (to make it work). JavaScript is a superior language to Java, from which it is entirely separate. Anyone who argues Java is better than JavaScript without referencing Minecraft is objectively wrong.
  • Q: How do you know like 10 languages?

However, I didn't just copy and paste the whole thing into Google Translate. I used the internet as a tool, but the translations are my own.
